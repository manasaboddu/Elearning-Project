JavaScript used to explain pythagorean theorem

Explanation

Pythagorean Theorem-One of the best known mathematical formulas, which provides the relationship between the sides of a right angeled triangle. A right angeled triangle consists of two sides and a hypotenuse.
To satify pythagorean theorm, the length of the hypotenuse should be equal to square root of the sum of the squares of the other two sides.

  • Here we have used JavaScript to check whether the sides of a triangle entered by user forms a right angeled triangle or not.
  • We have used "window.prompt" to accept input from a user.
  • Prompt is the method of window object and it facilitates the user to enter the values as shown in the example.
  • We have used the conditional statements-if..else to check whether the hypotenuse is equal to the square root of the sum of squares of the other two sides.
  • Then finally, we have displayed the result using "document.write".
